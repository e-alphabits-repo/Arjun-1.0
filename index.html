Privacy Policy for Arjun 1.0

Last Updated: June 11, 2026  ·  Effective Date: June 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how EAlphabits Tech Innovations ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your information when you use the Arjun 1.0 mobile application (the "App") together with your paired Arjun 1.0 AI Device (the "AI Device"). Together, the App and the AI Device make up the "Arjun 1.0 service."

This Privacy Policy is written for users and describes only the information practices that apply to the Arjun 1.0 service. It is intended to meet the requirements of the Google Play Store, the Apple App Store, the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA).

By using the Arjun 1.0 service, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Introduction

Arjun 1.0 is a smart monitoring and AI-based detection service. The App lets you set up and manage your AI Device, view live video from your AI Device, configure detection features, and receive alerts when an event is detected. This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the Arjun 1.0 service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to provide the Arjun 1.0 service.

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Name (required). You provide your name for your profile.
  • Phone number (required). You sign in using your mobile phone number and a one-time code (OTP) sent by SMS. Your phone number is your primary account identifier.
  • Email address (required). You must provide an email address for your profile.
  • Alert recipient phone numbers (optional). If you choose to enable WhatsApp alert messages, you may type a phone number or select a single contact to receive those alerts. Only the one phone number you choose is stored; we do not import, read, or store your full contact list.
  • Customer support content. When you contact support, we collect the message you send and any images or videos you choose to attach.

2.2 Information Collected Through Your Use of the App

  • Account information, used to create your account and keep you signed in.
  • AI Device information, such as the name and configuration of each AI Device you pair, so the App can manage it for you.
  • Alerts and alert images generated by your AI Device and made available to you in the App so you can review activity.
  • Notification token, used solely to deliver alerts and notifications to your phone.
  • Diagnostic and technical information. When you contact customer support, we include limited technical information with your request — such as your app version and your device model and operating system version — to help us resolve the issue. This information is used only for support and app functionality, and is not used for advertising. The App does not use any third-party analytics or crash-reporting services.

2.3 Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your location. The App does not request any location permission and does not access your GPS, network-based location, or any other location data.
  • We do not record or transmit microphone audio from your phone.
  • We do not use advertising identifiers, and we do not engage in advertising or marketing tracking.
  • We do not collect financial or payment information through the App.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account and sign you in.
  • Set up and manage your AI Device and its detection features.
  • Provide live video viewing and alerting.
  • Send you alerts and notifications when an event is detected.
  • Respond to your customer support requests.
  • Keep the service reliable and secure, including diagnosing errors.
  • Notify you about, and provide, app updates.
  • Comply with our legal obligations.

Legal bases (GDPR). Where GDPR applies, we process your information to provide the service you request, with your consent (for example, optional alert messages, and camera, photo, contacts, and notification permissions), for our legitimate interests (such as security and improving the service), and to comply with legal obligations.

4. AI Device Management Features

The App lets you manage your AI Device and its AI-based detection features, including crowd and capacity detection, footfall counting, restricted-area monitoring, suspicious-activity alerts, and absence detection. You control which features are enabled and can adjust their settings, including limiting detection to specific times.

When a detection occurs, the AI Device captures a single still image so you can review the event in the App, and you can choose to receive an alert. Live video is shown for viewing and is not continuously stored.

Because the AI Device captures images of physical spaces, you are responsible for ensuring that your use of the AI Device complies with all applicable laws, including any notice and consent requirements for monitoring people and premises.

5. Permissions: Camera, Media, and Contacts

  • Camera. With your permission, the App can use your phone's camera so you can capture a photo or short video to attach to a customer support request, and to scan a setup QR code while pairing your AI Device. The camera is used only when you actively choose to use it.
  • Saving images. With your permission, the App can save alert images to your phone's photo library or gallery.
  • Selecting media. When you attach images or videos to a support request, the App uses the system photo picker and accesses only the specific files you select. The App does not request broad photo or video library permissions.
  • Contacts. If you choose to add a WhatsApp alert recipient from your contacts, the App opens the system contact picker and reads only the single phone number you select.
  • Sharing. You can share alert images using your phone's standard sharing feature.

You can decline or revoke camera, photo, contacts, and notification permissions at any time in your device settings.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored securely using trusted service providers (including Google Firebase) and is protected by appropriate technical and organizational measures. Information transmitted between the App and our cloud services is sent over secure, encrypted (HTTPS/TLS) connections, and access is restricted so that you can only access your own account and the AI Devices paired to it. Some information may also be stored on your phone so the App can function.

To protect against abuse and fraud, the App uses Google Play Integrity (via Firebase App Check) to verify that requests to our backend come from a genuine, unmodified copy of the App. This check does not collect your personal information.

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure. While we use commercially reasonable safeguards to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

  • Account and AI Device information: kept while your account is active, until you delete your account.
  • Alerts and alert images: automatically deleted after approximately 60 days.
  • Customer support content: kept as needed to resolve and document your request.
  • Diagnostic logs: kept only as long as needed for reliability and support.
  • Information stored on your phone: kept until you sign out, delete your account, or uninstall the App.

When you delete your account, we remove your profile and unpair your AI Devices, as described in Section 11.

8. Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted third-party service providers — primarily Google Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Realtime Database, Cloud Messaging, and Cloud Storage) — to support and operate the App, including account sign-in, data storage, and notifications. These providers process information only as needed to perform their services on our behalf and are subject to their own privacy practices. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it with third parties for their own advertising or marketing purposes.

9. Children's Privacy

The Arjun 1.0 service is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or under 16 where a higher age applies under local law, such as the GDPR). If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at the email address in Section 12, and we will take steps to delete it.

By using the service, you understand that your information may be transferred internationally as described here.

California residents (CCPA/CPRA): you have the right to know what personal information we collect and how we use it, to request its deletion, to correct inaccurate information, and not to be discriminated against for exercising your rights. EEA/UK residents (GDPR): you may exercise the rights listed above and have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Account and Data Deletion

You can delete your account and associated data at any time, in two ways:

  • In the App: open Profile → Delete Account. This removes your profile, unpairs your AI Devices, deletes your data from our servers, and signs you out.
  • By request: email ealphabitstechinnovations@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion." After we verify your request, we will delete your account and associated data in accordance with applicable laws and our retention obligations.

When you delete your account, we remove your profile, unpair your AI Devices, and clear your information from the service. Certain information may be retained for a limited period where required to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements. We will respond within the timeframes required by applicable law and may need to verify your identity before fulfilling your request.
